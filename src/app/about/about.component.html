About Me

I’m an ardent Nature lover and a self-taught photographer from India, specialising in Wildlife and Nature Photography.I’m the 1st prize Winner of Natgeo Nature photographer of year 2016.

My works have been featured on Natgeo thrice and I have been on an expedition with Natgeo to Costa Rica and Panama as a result of winning a contest conducted by Natgeo in 2013. My photos have embellished book covers, jigsaw puzzles and the walls of several offices and homes.
